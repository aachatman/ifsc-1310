Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive enhancement focuses on creating content that operates sufficiently and effectively across different devices. In progressive enhancement, HTML serves as the fundamental layer that everything else is built. It ensures that users can access basic content no matter the browser or network. The goal is to ensure that even with no CSS or JavaScript, a user can still read and understand the information and navigate the site or complete essential tasks. It means a stable foundation was created before adding more complex and decorative coding. It is important because it enhances accessibility, improves performance, and can ensure universal access.
